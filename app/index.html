Enid Blyton

The Magic Faraway tree published in 1951 was one of the first novels I read. I particular liked this one because it made me imagine the existence of alternate worlds. Imagination can lead to discovery and that is exactly what Enid relied on. Every page in her book was full of adventure and I still enjoy going back and reading through them, reminiscing my beautiful childhood moments. Enid indeed mastered childrens literature, no doubt she was the best seller since 1930. She is even remembered today as the creator of Noddy, the writer of the famous series Secret Seven, Famous Five and Adventures of the wishing chair. The Magic Faraway Tree was a turning point for me. I believed someday I would create something as magical as this and sway people with my creation.

Hot Wheels - Next Gen

I tried to display my texturing skills through this project. My idea was to create a hot wheels car with a futuristic skin. I took my inspiration from a species called Umbonia Spinosa. Each object in the scene was UV mapped. PSD networks were created to apply texture and bump maps accordingly using Photoshop.
